Hello, my name is Subhra Chakraborti. I am from New Town, India. Currently, I am studying in CBSE Class XII. I have a diverse range of interests and hobbies, including music, football, movies, and chess. Although I don’t have professional experience or achievements yet, I am driven by a strong desire to become an astrophysicist in the future.

I believe in approaching life with a tough and rational mindset, always seeking logical solutions to challenges. I am fascinated by the mysteries of the universe and have a particular interest in astrophysics and space exploration.

Outside of academics, I enjoy playing musical instruments, engaging in friendly football matches, watching movies, and challenging my mind with a game of chess. These activities provide me with a sense of relaxation and inspiration.

My ultimate goal is to contribute to the field of astrophysics and unravel the secrets of the cosmos. I am dedicated to pursuing my education and acquiring the necessary skills to make a meaningful impact in this field.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me. I am excited about the journey ahead and look forward to the opportunities and experiences that await.
