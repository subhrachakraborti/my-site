SubhraVerse?
Subhra Chakraborti is an aspiring astrophysicist from New Town, India, currently studying in CBSE Class XII. With interests in music, football, movies, and chess, Subhra is driven by a passion for space exploration and a rational approach to life’s challenges, aiming to make significant contributions to astrophysics. SubhraVerse is an intelligent community founded by Subhra Chakraborti, aimed at fostering a strong fraternity among its members who are passionate about the Universe, Space, Astrophysics, and more. This community is designed to bring together people from around the globe, providing a platform for them to share their views and engage in meaningful discussions on a global scale. SubhraVerse is an online community with its main page hosted on Nas.Io, thanks to the support of my friend, Nusier Yassin. The community also features a blog on WordPress and offers a Telegram Channel and Group for discussions. Joining is free, and donations or sponsorships are always welcome.
